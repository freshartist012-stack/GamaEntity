GAMA Entity - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

GAMA Entity is developed by Listix, in collaboration with AfriTechX (Pty) Ltd. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

GAMA Entity stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your name (entered during onboarding)
  • Chat history (stored on your device only)
  • Your chosen AI model preference and API key (stored locally)

Permissions Used

  • Contacts — to look up phone numbers when you ask GAMA to call or message someone
  • Phone — to make calls on your behalf when requested
  • Microphone — for voice input only when you tap the mic button
  • Internet — to connect to your chosen AI provider

How Your Data Is Used

Your conversations are sent to whichever AI provider you select:

  • GAMA Native Model — conversations go to our server. We do not store or share them.
  • Groq API — conversations go to Groq's servers, subject to Groq's privacy policy.
  • Custom API — conversations go to the provider you configure.

Data Storage

Chat history and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

GAMA Entity is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
